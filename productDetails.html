Gloves

Cut Level 2 Nitrile Dipped Gloves

Our Cut Level 2 Nitrile Dipped Gloves are designed to be more durable and provide better protection. These cut-resistant gloves have a reinforced nitrile coating between the thumb and index finger for enhanced durability in high wear points. MILWAUKEE® SMARTSWIPE™ index fingertips provide you with full access to your touchscreen devices without removing the gloves, and the textured nitrile palm provides you with best-in-class grip performance. The cut level 2 gloves are knit with 15 gauge material for better dexterity when handling small objects. These gloves are made of a nylon and lycra blend to provide all-day comfort and wick away moisture.
